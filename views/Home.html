How it Works

  1. Create a redirect email from our site or extension
  2. Add any optional customizations (filters, modifiers, etc)
  3. Use the new redirect email we give you to sign up with a site or receive emails to
  4. Emails are sent to your Ptorx address
  5. Our system makes sure the incoming mail matches your provided filters
  6. Our system then applies any modifiers you specified, to the message
  7. The email is then redirected to your main email, or stored on Ptorx for later viewing

Privacy

Ptorx increases your privacy anywhere you use email by acting like a proxy for your email. How does it work? We allow you to create Ptorx email addresses that then redirect emails to your main email. Wherever you would use your email to sign up on a website or give out an email to be contacted at, you can now use a Ptorx redirect email. This means whoever receives your Ptorx address will not be able to get your real address.

Another benefit to Ptorx is that your real email will no longer end up on people search websites. Many sites you use end up selling your information, including your email to sites that allow you to find people's personal information. With Ptorx, if this does happen, your real email is still safe and no one will be able to link you to accounts on other websites where you used the same email.

Safe From Spam

If a spammer ever obtains one of your Ptorx addresses all you have to do is delete the Ptorx address and no more spam will be redirected to your main email. Additionally, you can utilize our filter system to filter out unwanted emails.

Improved Security

Let's say a website you use gets hacked and the hackers gain access to your account information. Now they have your email and will do anything possible to profit off of it. This means your address is sold to spammers if you're lucky, or is the target of further account theft if you're unlucky. An email is valuable to a hacker because they know if they can get access to it, they can get access to almost any account you've registered with that address. With Ptorx, they won't know what your real email is and therefore they can't attempt to access any of your other accounts. And of course, if they do start sending you spam or something else, adding a filter or just deleting the address will stop any spam before it gets to you.

Control Your Email

Our filter and modifier systems allow you to control your email like never before.

Filters

Filters allow you to determine what emails actually get redirected to your main address by checking for values in any messages received. They are a great way to prevent spam, phishing emails, scams, and other unwanted messages. Filters include subject, sender address, sender domain, text content, HTML content, and email header filters. You can configure your email to ignore messages where a filter matches, or only accept messages that match all provided filters. You can also utilize regular expressions for more advanced filters.

Modifiers

Modifiers allow you to modify an incoming message's content before it is redirected to your main address. Modifiers include content encryption, text only mode (removes HTML), find and replace, subject overwrite, and subject tag modifiers. Subject tag modifiers allow you to 'tag' any incoming emails by prepending or appending a string of text to the original email's subject.

Keep Your Email

Since Ptorx just redirects messages to you, you don't have to give up your existing address. You can still receive and view your messages in your normal email programs, apps, and websites.
